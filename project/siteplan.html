Site Plan: ROMIRA GLOBAL | Crochet Business Supplies

Site Name

ROMIRA GLOBAL | Crochet Business Toolkit

This name is my own registered company in angola with an underdeveloping site, focused on empowering Angolan artisans through a super high-quality supplies for crochet projects.

Domain: romiraglobal.ao (hypothetical)

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to introduce ROMIRA GLOBAL crochet business supplies. It helps artisans track inventory, estimate project costs, and generate receipts. The site also shares the story behind the supplies.

Scenarios (Crochet Supplies FAQ)

  • How can I track how much yarn I have left after each project?
  • Can I estimate the cost of a custom crochet item before I start?
  • What is the best way to manage my hook and accessory inventory?
  • How do I apply discount to customer orders?
  • Can I test the supplies before using it for real transactions?
